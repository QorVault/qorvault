## KEY FINDINGS

1. **Chronic absence crisis** — 33% of students chronically absent (67.1% regular attendance), ~20 points below pre-pandemic levels. 10th graders worst at 51.2%.

2. **Math proficiency gutted** — 32.9% proficient district-wide, down from ~48% pre-COVID. 6th grade only 26.7%.

3. **Demographic transformation** — Majority-minority district, no group above 27%. ELL population nearly doubled to 31.8% of enrollment.

4. **Teacher-student diversity gap** — 79.4% White teachers serving a 73.9% non-White student body.

5. **9th grade on-track collapse** — 61.6%, down from 73% pre-pandemic. Leading indicator for future graduation declines.

6. **Graduation equity gaps** — 34-point gap between Highly Capable (97.2%) and NHPI students (63.0%). ELL at 69.6%.

7. **Kindergarten readiness declining** — Math readiness at decade-low 56.6% in 2025-26.

8. **Class sizes growing** — Average jumped to 22.1 as teacher count fell 7.5%.

9. **Discipline spike** — 4.55% exclusion rate in 2023-24 (decade high), 6th grade at 7.76%.

10. **Bright spot: Dual credit** — 78.4% participation, a new high.
